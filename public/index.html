Why Attend

We are a passionate community of students interested in building software using proven and new technologies to build interesting projects. We typically host our events on Wednesdays at 4:00PM in Peralta 213 at the ASU Polytechnic campus. Attend one of our events by looking through the calendar below!

Workshops

We host hands-on workshops covering topic such as web development, iOS development, android development, AR/VR, and many more.

Interview Practice

Fun and collaborative whiteboarding sessions were members will get into small groups and solve problems presented by our club organizers. Topics include data structures, algorithms, OOP concepts, operating system concepts, and networking.

Industry Outreach

We host talks from industry professionals, some of which are alumnus from this club, from different companies in which students can learn about real world software development.

Dev Club Community

In addition to guest speakers, workshops, and interview practices, the main goal of the club is to provide a safe and collaborative environment for aspiring developers.
